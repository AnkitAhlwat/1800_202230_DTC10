About Us

Breakly is a concept 3 students had to encourage people to take a break from their work or schoolwork. We hope by using our web application it encourages you to take some time to yourself and avoid burnout.

FAQ

Anyone and everyone! We believe with work from home being so prominent, it makes it very challenging for people to disconnect from work. Hopefully with these little reminders it will motivate you to step away and take a break.

After you create an account, you login each morning and set the interval of how often you'd like to log a break as well as your personal goal for the day. The break timer counts down the minutes until your next scheduled break, and at that scheduled break time, you log your break. Every time you log a break and get closer and closer to your personal goal, your plant grows more and more!
